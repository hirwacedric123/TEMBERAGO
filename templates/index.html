Our Story

Born in Rwanda, Built for the World

TemberaGo was founded with a single vision: to show the world Rwanda's extraordinary beauty through premium, seamlessly managed travel experiences. What began as a small executive transport service in Kigali has grown into Rwanda's most comprehensive travel management company.

We believe every traveller deserves more than a ride — they deserve an experience. Our team of passionate Rwandans brings deep cultural knowledge, professional training, and genuine warmth to every journey.

Trust & SafetyAll vehicles are fully insured, tracked, and maintained to international standards.
Luxury & ComfortFrom spotless interiors to cold water and WiFi — we obsess over every detail.
Local ExpertiseOur guides and drivers know Rwanda intimately — from secret viewpoints to the best local restaurants.
SustainabilityWe support eco-tourism and community initiatives that protect Rwanda's environment.
